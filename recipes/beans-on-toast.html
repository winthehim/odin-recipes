Beans on toast

Description

A Bri'ish staple. Beans on toast!
Serves: 1
Cooking time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • Can of baked beans
  • Butter for spreading
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Toast your bread to your liking
  2. Empty can of beans into microwave safe bowl and cook until piping hot
  3. Butter toast when ready
  4. Pour the lovely hot beans on your toast
  5. God save the King!!!!
