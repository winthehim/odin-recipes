Fish pie

Description

Fish head pie!!! Just like me nan used to make
Serves: 6
Cooking time: 2 hours (Give or take)

Ingredients

  • 5 fish heads
  • Homepride white sauce
  • Puff pastry sheet (premade)

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 180deg fan/200deg gas
  2. Fill a casserole dish with the jar of Homepride sauce
  3. Lay sheet of puff pastry on top
  4. Stick fish heads, mouth side up, into the puff pasty
  5. Bake in oven for 1 hour 45 minutes
  6. Take a bite and have a ratatouille flashback moment :)
