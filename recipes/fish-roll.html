Fish roll!

Description

A sausage roll but instead of mystery meat, you get a whole organic fish!
Serves: 3
Cooking time: 40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large organic mackerel
  • Puff pastry (premade)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180deg fan/ 200deg gas
  2. Lay puff pastry sheet out
  3. Place whole organic mackerel in the centre of pastry
  4. Fold ends of the pastry over the fish to create a roll
  5. Place on a baking tray and into the oven for 25 minutes
  6. Serve hot or cold
  7. Cheeky fish roll, perfect for lunch! Yum!
